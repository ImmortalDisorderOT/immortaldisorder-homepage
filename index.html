About

Immortal Disorder originally was called Immortal Order and was a guild that played multiple "Open Tibia" servers. The original leaders went on to create their own server, BlackTalon Online while the rest of the group played the server and rebranded to the new generation of the guild, Immortal Disorder

Today, the guild plays a variety of Open Tibia servers and other games and has grown to over 50 active members.
